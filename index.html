The Numberbender Difference

Built for the flipped classroom.
Designed for global reach.

Dr. E pioneered flipped classroom math at the community college level — students watch the lesson before class, then use class time for deeper problem-solving. That same asynchronous model now reaches learners in 10+ countries who've never set foot in his classroom.

  • 🏠
    Watch at home, practice in class

    Every video is designed to replace lecture time — concise, complete, and ready to assign before the next session.

  • 🌐
    Bilingual for Filipino STEM students worldwide

    Lessons in English and Filipino (Tagalog) — built for students in the Philippines, UAE, Canada, and every corner of the Filipino diaspora.

  • 📱
    Asynchronous by design

    Pause, rewind, replay at 2AM before an exam — the flexibility traditional lecture never gave students.

Meet the founder

Dr. Peter Esperanza — Math Professor & Online Educator

Dr. E is a community college math professor, published researcher, online educator, content creator, and conference presenter. He founded Numberbender on a simple belief: that democratizing education means making great math instruction free, accessible, and available to anyone — student or teacher, anywhere in the world.

Through social media, he has reached hundreds of thousands of learners and shared best practices in online teaching, flipped learning, AI integration, and educational technology.
