🔒 Your privacy matters

Privacy Policy

Numberbender is committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Last updated: May 2026

Numberbender Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes — ever.

Who we are

Numberbender is operated by Numberbender Inc., a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization founded by Dr. Peter Esperanza. Our website is located at numberbender.com. For any privacy-related questions, you can reach us at peter@numberbender.com.

What information we collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide through our contact form. This includes:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your school or organization (optional)
  • The topic of your inquiry
  • Your message

We do not collect any payment information, social security numbers, or other sensitive personal data. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

How we use your information

The information you submit through our contact form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Follow up on speaking, workshop, or collaboration requests
  • Answer student or educator questions

We do not use your information for any automated marketing, profiling, or advertising purposes.

How your information is stored

Contact form submissions are processed and stored by Netlify, the platform that hosts this website. Netlify's privacy policy is available at netlify.com. Dr. E receives form submissions directly by email and retains them only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry.

Do we share your information?

No. We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third parties for commercial purposes. Your information is used only to respond to you directly.

Third-party services

This website embeds content from and links to the following third-party platforms:

  • YouTube — for hosting math and teaching videos
  • TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram — for social media content

When you visit or interact with content on those platforms, their own privacy policies apply. Numberbender does not control those services.

Children's privacy

Our website is intended for students, educators, and professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has submitted information through our contact form, please email us at peter@numberbender.com and we will promptly delete it.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you have submitted to us. To make such a request, contact us at peter@numberbender.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
