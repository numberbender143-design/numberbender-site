Meet the founder

Dr. Peter Esperanza — Math Professor & Online Educator

Dr. E is a community college math professor, published researcher, online educator, content creator, and conference presenter. He founded Numberbender on a simple belief: that democratizing education means making great math instruction free, accessible, and available to anyone — student or teacher, anywhere in the world.

Through social media, he has reached hundreds of thousands of learners and shared best practices in online teaching, flipped learning, AI integration, and educational technology.
