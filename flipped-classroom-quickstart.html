Which unit to flip first

Not a random choice — a specific rule for identifying the unit where flipping will have the biggest immediate impact in your class.

Video length rules

One concept per video. 5–10 minutes max. The analytics-backed rules that separate videos students actually finish from videos they abandon at the four-minute mark.

5 mistakes to avoid
