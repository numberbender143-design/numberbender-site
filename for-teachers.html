Why teachers trust Dr. E

He didn't just study online teaching. He built it from scratch.

In 2012, Dr. Peter Esperanza started recording math videos for his high school algebra students after school. He had no studio, no following, and no roadmap. He just knew his students needed something better than the textbook.

Over the next decade he recorded 800+ YouTube lessons, earned a Silver Play Button, presented his research at conferences in Europe and Asia, trained hundreds of educators, and built a nonprofit to make sure everything stayed free.

When he teaches educators how to teach online, he's not sharing theory. He's sharing the exact system he built and tested in his own classroom for over 20 years.
